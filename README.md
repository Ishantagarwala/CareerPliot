# Brainware AI Hackathon 2026

Welcome to the repository for the **Brainware AI Hackathon 2026**, jointly organized by the **AIRDC Cell** and the **Tech Club** of Brainware University. The hackathon aims to foster innovation, creativity, and problem-solving abilities by encouraging the development of impactful AI-based solutions.

---

## 📅 Key Dates & Timeline

| Event Phase | Date / Timeline |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Proposal Submission Deadline** | **June 22, 2026** |
| **Prototype Screening** | 2nd Week of July 2026 |
| **Prototype Shortlisting** | 4th Week of July 2026 |
| **Prototype Presentation** | August 2026 (Before an expert panel; exact date TBA in July) |

---

## 📋 Guidelines & Eligibility

* **Team Composition:** Maximum 5 students per team.
* **Eligibility:** Open to students from all Schools and Departments of Brainware University.
* **Shortlist Selection:** 20 – 30 teams will be invited for the final prototype presentations in August 2026.
* **Rewards for Top 3 Teams:** The top 3 winning teams will earn direct qualification for participation in an external hackathon.

## 🏆 Awards & Recognition

* **Total Cash Prize:** **₹2.5 Lakhs (2 Lakhs & fifty thousand)** for the winners.
* **Recognition:** Certificates and institutional recognition.
* **Participation:** All shortlisted teams will receive participation certificates.
* **Mentorship:** Winning projects will receive expert mentorship for further development and implementation.

---

## 💡 Note for Brainware AI Hackathon 2025 Finalists

Interested finalists of the **Brainware AI Hackathon 2025** (excluding the winning teams) will receive **direct entry** into the Prototype Evaluation Round for the 2026 Hackathon, provided they demonstrate significant innovations, enhancements, or advancements in the prototypes previously showcased during the 2025 edition.
